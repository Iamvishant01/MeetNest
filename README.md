# Meetnest

**Meetnest** is a powerful and versatile video conferencing platform designed to bring seamless virtual communication to individuals, teams, and organizations. Whether it's for meetings, webinars, or online events, Meetnest ensures a smooth, secure, and high-quality experience across all devices.

---

## 🚀 Features

- **HD Video Calls**: Experience crystal-clear, high-definition video for meetings.
- **Screen Sharing**: Easily share your screen for presentations or collaborations.
- **Real-Time Collaboration**: Chat, share files, and work together during live sessions.
- **Cross-Device Support**: Works smoothly on desktops, laptops, and mobile devices.
- **Secure and Encrypted**: Your calls and meetings are protected with end-to-end encryption.
- **Customizable Layouts**: Choose from different video layouts based on your needs.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Intuitive and easy-to-use, no steep learning curve required.

---

## 🎯 Use Cases

- **Team Meetings**: Stay connected and productive with your remote or hybrid teams.
- **Webinars**: Host live webinars, engage with your audience through interactive tools.
- **Online Events**: Organize virtual events, conferences, and workshops.
- **1-on-1 Calls**: Conduct personal or professional 1-on-1 calls effortlessly.

---

## 💻 Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: ReactJS
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js
- **Video Integration**: WebRTC , Socket.IO
